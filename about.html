About

I'm a Theoretical Physicist by training, with interests in biophysics, condensed matter physics, systems biology, and computational methods. I'm particularly interested in deriving fundamental design principles of biological systems. This includes understanding the structure and flow of information in biological systems, and understanding how biology leverages stochasticity for achieving desired functionality.

Outside of Physics, I'm a Carnatic classical Vocalist and a Veena player. I love learning new languages, and reading fiction and history. I have a podcast, Ninaada: The Waves of Music exploring the connections between music, cultures, and neuroscience.
